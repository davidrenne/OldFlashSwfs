tele=The Telephone Building was built in 1910.  It was designed by Story and Van Egmond of Regina.  Bell Telephone cut the original line to Moose Jaw in 1883.
The earliest phone call recorded in Saskatchewan took place in Moose Jaw in 1885 when Sioux Indians working in an intelligence capacity called Wood Mountain to warn about a possible raid from America during the Riel uprising.
Automatic service began in 1919 and for rural customers in 1949.
