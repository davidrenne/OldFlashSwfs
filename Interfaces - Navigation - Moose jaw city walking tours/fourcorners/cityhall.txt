cityhall=Construction on the Post Office began November 25, 1911.  Faced with Tyndall stone, it features a mansard roof and clock tower.  This building is an example of turn-of-the-century government architecture.  Through the years, it housed various government offices until obtained by the City in 1965.
The walls of City Hall also feature a wide assortment of fossils imbedded in the Tyndall stone.
