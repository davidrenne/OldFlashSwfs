eatons=This building was originally the 1928 C. and W. Grayson Building.  Built on the site of the William Grayson’s second 1891 house.  R.G. Bunyard and Symonds of Moose Jaw designed the building.  In 1940, a one story annex was added.  Eaton’s introduced the concept of national chain department stores to Moose Jaw.
The Eaton’s Building is now a seniors housing project, titled Timothy Eaton Gardens.
