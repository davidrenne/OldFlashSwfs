granthall=The Grant Hall Hotel was built in 1928 and was named after the Vice President of the Canadian Pacific Railway as part of the CPR Chain of Hotels.
In 1934, the Grant Hall was the original home of 10 AB, the forerunner to CHAB.  Live broadcasting was from the west wing of the 4th floor.
In 1939, the Hotel was visited by the late King George VI and the Queen Mother.  Other celebrities that have visited the hotel include Louis (Sachmo) Armstrong, Mart Kenny and his Western Gentlemen, Charles Laughton, Roy Rogers, “Rocket” Richard, Pierre Burton and John Diefenbaker.
