artmuseum=The Moose Jaw Art Museum was built as a 1967 Centennial Project for the citizens of Moose Jaw.
The Art Museum is a National Exhibition Centre with two galleries, able to provide temporary exhibit space for local, provincial, national and international exhibitions.  The downstairs features the Heritage Gallery with artifacts that relate to the local history of Moose Jaw and District.
Three thousand historic artifacts and over 400 art works comprise the Art Museum’s permanent collection.
