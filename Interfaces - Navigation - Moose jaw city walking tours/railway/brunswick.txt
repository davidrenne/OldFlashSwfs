brunswick=John Henry Kern (owner of the Maple Leaf) built the Brunswick Hotel in 1903.  It was constructed of Moose Jaw Wellington White red brick.  The south wall featured a double deck veranda.  Its extra long bar made the Brunswick famous.  It was reputed to be “one of the finest hotels west of Winnipeg”.
The City of Moose Jaw was incorporated in this building during a celebration November 20, 1903.
Today the Brunswick Hotel is a drinking establishment with scheduled live entertainment.
