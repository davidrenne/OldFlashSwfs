empress=The Empress Hotel was built in 1911 and was one of Moose Jaw’s most elegant establishments.  It was a four story brick building with stone facings.  It contained a sweeping staircase and elevator.  One half of the rooms had their own private bathrooms.  It was built for $85,000.
One legend associated with the Empress is that Al Capone was once a registered guest although no evidence has ever been found of this.
The Empress was destroyed by fire in 1987.
