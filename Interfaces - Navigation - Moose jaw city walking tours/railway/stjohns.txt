johns=St. John’s Anglican Church was built in 1909.  It contains one of the finest collections of stained glass windows in Western Canada and is famous for its cathedral style interior.
This church was used for the first time on Easter Day, March 27, 1910.
The 72 foot tower has a peal of eight bells, the largest weighing over 1900 pounds.  It also includes the 1888 Children’s Bell from the original St. John’s on River Street East.
Note the tile, from the chancel of the church in Chiselhurst, England, above the west entrance.
