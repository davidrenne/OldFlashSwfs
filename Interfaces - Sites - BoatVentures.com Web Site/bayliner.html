Bayliner.com

 

. BoatVentures.com launched Bayliner.com live to the public September 29th 2000
. Bayliner.com is one of the Internet's larger data base driven web sites complete with custom developed software including back end administration tools for the client to add and remove various boat models and technical information including drawings and photos.
. Custom software includes Comprehensive Boat Search Engine, Lead Generation Forms, Lead Sorting and Distribution, Local Dealer Locator and an Inference Engine to help users choose the type of boat best suited for them.
